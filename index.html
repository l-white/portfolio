Roadmap to Readiness Website

This website was created in just two short weeks and involves a two-woman team: my friend and colleague Shelly Keller, a talented database administrator who manages the back end, and yours truly, who took an existing Wordpress theme and modified it to fit Milwaukee Public Schools style and branding considerations.

The website was created to better support the educational needs of the 75,000 K-8 public school children in Milwaukee and their families during the Covid-19 pandemic when virtual learning was necessary.

Check out the Roadmap to Readiness website.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Wordpress, PHP

Diederich College of Communication Website

Migrating the Diederich College of Communication website for Marquette University is one of the largest web development projects I have undertaken to date. The website was originally 500 pages. The migration process included re-writing and re-structuring the website so that it was down to 350 webpages and every webpage could be accessed within one level of the website. I also re-wrote the meta descriptions to improve SEO. I was part of a 10-person team at Marquette, selected to participate in this process while keeping up with my other job responsibilities as multimedia manager.

With the exception of some changes to the navigation bar and homepage, most of the web designs I implemented are still intact.

Check out the Diederich College of Communication website.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, XML
